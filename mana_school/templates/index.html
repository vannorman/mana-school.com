Prepare For Life

At Mana, we believe in the maximal empowerment of the individual, through teaching skills that will enable, empower, accelerate, and energize our students.

Why Mana

Traditional education - to sit, listen, memorize, and test - do not prepare you to succeed in the real world. We test the bounds of what is possible in transferring the expertise and wisdom of our mentors to our students.

Mentor / Facilitator model

All of us have had great teachers; but you won't find that role at Mana. Instead, we cultivate mentors and facilitators to enable and cultivate a garden of learning and possibility for our students. Ultimately, our students decide their journey.

Our model

We offer 1-1 or group sessions for specific subjects. We are highly customized to each learner's needs; we also offer strong foundations if there is no pre-determined subject matter. As Ghandi once said, If you do not know your life's purpose, then your purpose is to find your purpose. If not, then we'll do our best to prepare our students to take the best next steps they can.
